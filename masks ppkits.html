How masks, PPE kits and face shields are made?

Masks

Masks are made on a machine line that assembles the nonwovens from bobbins, ultrasonically welds the layers together, and stamps the masks with nose strips, ear loops, and other pieces. Completed masks are then sterilized before being sent out of the factory.

PPE Kits

PPE kits (Persoal Protective Equipment)are like a suit give to doctors when the visit the covid 19 patients.These kits include goggles, face shields, masks, gloves, coveralls or gowns, head covers and shoe covers.All those are made of plastic and reduce the risk of the person using them from contracting an infection.Polypropylene, a non-woven material that can be used only once, is largely used in making PPE kits.

Face Sheild

Face sheilds has two parts lens plate and headband.Lens plate is made of Polycarbonate which is the material of choice for many helmet-style face shields because of its optical clarity and impact resistance.Headband's design is made to hold the lens plate and adjust it with the lenght of 1-in.
