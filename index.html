The 8 RE's of Green Management

At one point or another we’ve all heard about the "EIGHT RE’s” of green managment:-

  • REduce
  • REuse
  • REcycle
  • REfuse
  • REpair
  • REthink
  • REporpose
  • REnovate

The goal of these eight RE’s is to prevent waste and conserve natural resources. We should implementing such “green” practices as the eight RE’s.
